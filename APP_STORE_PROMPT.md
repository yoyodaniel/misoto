# App Store Description Prompt for ChatGPT

Copy and paste this prompt into ChatGPT to generate App Store required texts:

---

**Prompt:**

I need you to write App Store listing texts for my iOS app called "Misoto". Please create the following:

1. **App Name**: Misoto
2. **Subtitle** (30 characters max)
3. **Description** (up to 4000 characters)
4. **Keywords** (100 characters max, comma-separated)
5. **Promotional Text** (170 characters max, optional)
6. **What's New** (for version updates, up to 4000 characters)

**App Overview:**
Misoto is a recipe sharing and discovery app that helps users create, organize, and share amazing recipes with a global community of food lovers.

**Key Features:**

**Recipe Creation:**
- Manual recipe entry with step-by-step instructions
- AI-powered recipe extraction from photos (take a photo of a dish and extract the recipe)
- AI-powered recipe extraction from links (extract recipes from recipe websites)
- AI-powered recipe extraction from websites (extract recipes from any website URL)
- Support for multiple images per recipe (up to 5 images)
- Step-by-step instructions with optional images and videos
- Ingredient organization (dish ingredients, marinade, seasoning, dough/batter/filling)
- Recipe metadata: prep time, cook time, servings, difficulty level, spicy level
- Cuisine categorization
- Tips and notes

**Recipe Management:**
- Save unlimited recipes (with Premium subscription)
- Free tier: 15 recipes maximum
- Organize recipes in your personal collection
- Edit and update recipes anytime
- Recipe privacy settings (Public, Private to All, Private Sharing)
- Share recipes with specific users via private sharing

**AI-Powered Features:**
- Extract recipes from photos using AI vision
- Extract recipes from recipe website links
- Extract recipes from any website URL
- Automatic ingredient detection and parsing
- Automatic instruction extraction
- Automatic time estimation (prep time, cook time)
- Automatic difficulty level detection
- Automatic spicy level detection
- Automatic cuisine type detection
- Recipe translation support (20 languages)
- Free tier: 5 AI extractions per month
- Premium: Unlimited AI extractions

**Social Features:**
- Discover recipes from the global community
- Follow other users
- Like/favorite recipes
- View user profiles with recipe counts, followers, following
- Explore trending and popular recipes
- Recipe notes and comments (coming soon)

**Localization:**
- Supports 20 languages: English, Chinese (Simplified & Traditional), Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Dutch, Russian, Japanese, Korean, Thai, Vietnamese, Indonesian, Malay, Filipino, Hindi, Arabic, Hebrew
- Automatic device language detection
- Full UI translation in all supported languages

**Premium Subscription:**
- Monthly: $4.99/month
- Yearly: $49.99/year (save 17%)
- Unlimited recipe storage (free tier: 15 recipes max)
- Unlimited AI extractions (free tier: 5 per month)
- All premium features unlocked

**Free Tier Limits:**
- 15 recipes maximum
- 5 AI extractions per month (resets on the 1st of each month)
- Manual recipe entry always available (as long as recipe limit not reached)

**Target Audience:**
- Home cooks and food enthusiasts
- People who want to discover new recipes
- Users who want to digitize their recipe collections
- People who want to share family recipes
- International users (20 language support)

**Tone & Style:**
- Friendly and inviting
- Focus on the joy of cooking and sharing
- Emphasize ease of use and AI-powered convenience
- Highlight the global community aspect
- Professional but approachable

**App Store Requirements:**
- Description should be engaging and highlight key features
- Keywords should be relevant and searchable
- Subtitle should be catchy and descriptive
- Promotional text should create urgency or highlight new features
- What's New should focus on recent improvements and features

Please write all texts in English, and make them compelling, clear, and optimized for App Store search and conversion.
